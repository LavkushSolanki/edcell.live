Ad Mad Show

Description

This national online event allows students from across India to participate in creating advertisements. Participants will be assigned topics in advance and must showcase their creativity and videography skills. A panel of judges will select the best submissions.

Rules of Procedure

  • Team Composition: Teams should consist of 5 members
  • Eligibility: Open to teams from colleges across India.
  • Event Structure: Each team will be given 4 brand options and must select one to create a 60-second advertisement video and prepare a presentation explaining the brand and ad concept.
  • Submission: Teams must submit the ad video (MP4, max 200 MB) and presentation (PPT) by the given deadline.
  • Final Presentation: Teams will present their ad and concept in a 6 minute online session via Google Meet or Zoom, followed by a brief Q&A with judges. etc.
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity, brand relevance, clarity and presentation skills.

Guidelines

  • Content Development: Ads should be creative, engaging, and align with the brand’s message within the 60-second limit.
  • Concept & Strategy: Ensure the ad is practical, impactful, and effectively communicates the brand’s value.
  • Time Management: Manage your time wisely to complete both the ad and presentation on time.
  • Presentation Preparation: Prepare a clear, concise pitch that explains your ad concept and strategy, using visual aids to enhance your presentation.
  • Requirements: All content must be original, ethical, and plagiarism free. No team is allowed to use any content against a rival brand in their ad or presentation. Judges' decisions are final.
